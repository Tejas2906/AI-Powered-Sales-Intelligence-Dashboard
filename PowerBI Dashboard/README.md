## 📊 Dashboard Overview

The **AI-Powered Sales Intelligence Dashboard** (built using Power BI) provides a comprehensive and interactive view of business performance, enabling stakeholders to make data-driven decisions quickly.

---

### 🖥️ Dashboard Preview

![Dashboard Overview](https://github.com/Tejas2906/AI-Powered-Sales-Intelligence-Dashboard/blob/73e2dc715aa6be3ea2741f26d7c8cdeb29eada8b/Screenshots/Dashboard.png)

---

### 🎯 Purpose

The dashboard is designed to:

- Transform complex sales data into **easy-to-understand visuals**
- Provide **real-time business insights**
- Help identify **trends, patterns, and anomalies**
- Support **strategic decision-making**

---

### 🧩 Key Dashboard Sections

#### 1️⃣ KPI Summary Panel

- 💰 Total Revenue  
- 📦 Total Orders  
- 📈 Sales Growth (%)  
- 🛒 Average Order Value  

👉 Provides a quick snapshot of overall business performance.

---

#### 2️⃣ Sales Trend Analysis

- 📊 Line charts showing sales over time (daily/monthly/yearly)
- 📉 Identifies growth trends and seasonal patterns

👉 Helps in forecasting and performance tracking.

---

#### 3️⃣ Region-wise Performance

- 🌍 Sales distribution by region
- 🗺️ Map visualization for geographic insights

👉 Identifies high-performing and underperforming regions.

---

#### 4️⃣ Product & Category Insights

- 🛍️ Top-selling products  
- 📦 Category-wise revenue contribution  
- 📊 Product performance comparison  

👉 Helps optimize inventory and marketing strategies.

---

#### 5️⃣ AI-Generated Insights Panel 🤖

- 📌 Automated summary of key findings  
- ⚠️ Alerts for unusual trends or drops  
- 🏆 Highlights top-performing segments  

👉 Reduces manual analysis effort using AI-driven intelligence.

![AI Generated Summary](https://github.com/Tejas2906/AI-Powered-Sales-Intelligence-Dashboard/blob/0e8b7a8d4e62a21cd59664662f2b8869c2b97a5a/Screenshots/AI%20Bussiness%20Summary.png)

---

#### 6️⃣ Filters & Interactivity

- 📅 Date Range Filter  
- 🌍 Region Filter  
- 📦 Category Filter  

👉 Enables users to drill down into specific data segments.
